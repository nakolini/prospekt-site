Privacy Policy

Effective: March 22, 2026

Overview

Prospekt is a camera lens simulator that helps you visualize how different focal lengths look in real time. Your privacy is important to us — the app is designed to work entirely on your device.

Data We Collect

None. Prospekt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising frameworks, or third-party services.

Camera Access

Prospekt requires access to your device's camera to display the live viewfinder. Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or shared unless you explicitly capture a photo. Captured photos are saved only to your device's photo library.

Local Preferences

Your settings (such as selected focal length, crop factor, and aspect ratio) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Prospekt does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new effective date.
